GALAXY

Journey Through Cosmos

This project was created to support children's learning activities. Many children tend to feel bored when faced with traditional learning visuals such as static images in books or on the internet. To address this, we offer a new, interactive learning medium using Augmented Reality (AR).


What is AR?

Augmented Reality (AR) is a technology that overlays digital elements—such as 3D models, animations, or information—onto the real world through a device like a smartphone or tablet. Unlike Virtual Reality, which creates an entirely virtual environment, AR enhances the real world by adding engaging and interactive content.

By integrating AR into education, we aim to make learning more immersive, fun, and effective. Children can explore concepts in a hands-on way, interact with learning materials, and stay more engaged throughout the process. This innovative approach not only helps students better understand the material but also encourages curiosity and active participation.
